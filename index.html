What is SDG 17?

Revitalize the Global Partnership for Sustainable Development

SDG 17 strengthens global cooperation between governments, the private sector, and civil society to achieve the ambitious goals of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development.

  • 🌍

    Global Cooperation

    Linking 193 nations in unified action toward common goals

  • 💰

    Financial Support

    Mobilizing resources from developed to developing nations

  • ⚙️

    Technology Sharing

    Transferring green innovation and digital infrastructure globally

  • 📊

    Data Exchange

    Open data systems for evidence-based policy and accountability
